Gap times are estimated from bus spacing and observed speed; routes with fewer than 3 active buses in a direction are excluded from gap calculations, and estimates are capped at 60 minutes. Bunching is detected when two buses on the same route/direction are within 250m. Late-night and low-service periods will show fewer metrics.
